### 1.2 Design Manifesto

> **"Your money, clearly seen. Your future, vividly imagined. Your path, confidently walked."**

Ikpa's design should make users feel:

| Emotion | How We Achieve It |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Confident** | Clear metrics, achievable progress, positive framing |
| **In Control** | Intuitive navigation, predictable interactions, no surprises |
| **Hopeful** | Future visualization, progress celebration, growth stories |
| **Supported** | Warm AI interactions, contextual guidance, no judgment |
| **Capable** | Simple explanations, actionable insights, achievable steps |

### 2.3 Design Style: "African Glassmorphism"

Our signature style combines:

1. **Glassmorphism Foundation**
   - Frosted glass effects for cards and overlays
   - Subtle transparency creating depth
   - Layered interfaces with visual hierarchy

2. **Warm Minimalism**
   - Clean layouts with generous white space
   - Purposeful use of color
   - Content-focused design

3. **African-Inspired Accents**
   - Color palette inspired by African landscapes
   - Subtle geometric patterns from African textiles
   - Typography with warmth and character

4. **Motion & Life**
   - Smooth, purposeful animations
   - Micro-interactions that delight
   - Progress celebrations
